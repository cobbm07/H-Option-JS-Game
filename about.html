About Me

My name is Michael Cobb and I am an aspiring Game Developer. I am pursuing a major in Computer Science as well as a secondary major in Media Arts and Game Design. I am in my third year of college, and in this time I have created a wide range of projects using a variety of programming languages and formats. Some of these projects were course requirements, but a majority of them have been Honors Options projects that I chose to take on independent of the course structure.

Along this journey, I have logged each of my projects to my github page. If you enjoy what I have created on this website, you may enjoy some of my other projects. Thank you for your support!
